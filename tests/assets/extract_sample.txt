Project Name: Lotus Path Martial Arts

Executive Summary:
Lotus Path Martial Arts is a community-focused dojo offering Brazilian jiu-jitsu, kickboxing, and youth programs.
We target families and working professionals seeking structured training and wellness. The model blends memberships,
small-group instruction, and seasonal camps to deliver predictable recurring revenue and positive cash flow after year one.

Financial Projections:
Startup Costs (one-time):
- Lease deposit & first month: $16,000
- Build-out & tatami/mats: $28,000
- Equipment & pads: $7,500
- Insurance: $2,400
- Signage & furniture: $3,100
- Permits/LLC/legal: $1,750
- Initial marketing: $4,200
- Working capital: $6,000
Total startup costs: $68,950

Projected Revenue Year 1: $180,000–$210,000 (target $195,000) driven by ~180 members at ~$95 average monthly revenue plus add-ons.
Projected Revenue Year 3: $320,000–$380,000 (target $350,000) assuming ~320 members, expanded youth classes, seminars, and camps.

Return on Investment:
Break-even expected around month 18; full payback by month 24.

Facility Details:
- 3,000 sq ft, street-level unit at Rivergate Plaza
- Two mat spaces (1,600 sq ft primary, 600 sq ft secondary)
- Lobby/retail area, staff office
- Two changing rooms and two restrooms
- Storage closet, 8 dedicated parking spaces
- ADA compliant; HVAC upgraded in 2024
- Operating hours: Mon–Fri 11:00–21:00; Sat 09:00–13:00
